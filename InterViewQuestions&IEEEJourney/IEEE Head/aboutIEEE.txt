Who is IEEE? 
	- Abbreviation for Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. 
the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology.
the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

When it has been founded? 
	- In the spring of 1884, a small group of individuals in the electrical professions met in New York
, USA. They formed a new organization to support professionals in their nascent field 
and to aid them in their efforts to apply innovation for the betterment of humanity.
